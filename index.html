What I Do

Everyone’s life is full of celebrations and so as mine. I always cherish my presence in any kind of celebrations being a Host or guest or any kind of participant. Here I am with Henna Art to make my presence memorable in your event. I am a Professional Henna Artist for your any special events like Birthday party, Graduation, Engagement, Bridal shower, Bridal Henna, Wedding Henna party or as simple as Ladies Night out, Belly blessing henna for to be mom. Henna on individual’s hand is used to adorn yourself. however, henna on any object can be decorative piece to enhance the beauty of your home. With the appreciation and demand from our community, I transferred Henna art from hands to candles, frames, plates or any objects and take custom orders. Please visit the website for my work and book your appointment to add touch of Indian traditions in your event. .
